Welcome to Mott Haven Fridge

Feeding the Neighborhood

Founded by two 6th-grade teachers in response to witnessing widespread food insecurity among their students' families The fridges are stocked by donations from individuals, local businesses and community partners and cleaned and maintained by a grassroots network of community volunteers With accolades from Bronxnet, News12, NY1, ABC7, Pix 11, WNYC, and CNN, the Mott Haven Fridge is a lifeline and beacon of hope for a community plagued by food insecurity.
